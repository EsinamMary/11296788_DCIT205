About Us

The Computer Science Students Association (COMPSSA) is a community of students dedicated to enhancing the experience of computer science students at the University of Ghana. We organize events, provide academic resources, and act as a liaison between students and the Computer Science Department.

Our Vision

  • To create an inclusive environment where every student feels welcome.
  • To foster a culture of collaboration and innovation.
  • To be a leading student association in the field of computer science.

Our Mission

  • To provide academic support and resources to our members.
  • To organize events that promote learning and networking.
  • To represent the interests of computer science students to the faculty and administration.
